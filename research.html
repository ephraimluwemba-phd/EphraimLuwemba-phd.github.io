PHD –– Charlotte's Web: assessing emerging industry guidelines in age-appropriate design

My PhD proposal is pending approval (granted July 2020) and thus subject to change. The broad focus of my project will be to help to guide the development of industry standards and guidelines for age appropriate design by employing methods of co-creation and practice-informed research on the guidelines that currently exist (E.g., the ICO's Age-appropriate Design Code).
